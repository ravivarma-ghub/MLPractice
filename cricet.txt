 100 Cricket Facts
History
Cricket originated in England in the 16th century.
The first international match was played in 1844 (USA vs Canada).
The first official Test match was played in 1877 (Australia vs England).
The Ashes started in 1882.
The ICC was founded in 1909.
The first Cricket World Cup was held in 1975.
West Indies won the first two World Cups (1975, 1979).
India won its first World Cup in 1983.
T20 International cricket started in 2005.
The first T20 World Cup was in 2007.
World Cup Records
Sachin Tendulkar scored the most World Cup runs.
Rohit Sharma has the most centuries in a single World Cup (5 in 2019).
Glenn McGrath has the most World Cup wickets (71).
Kapil Dev scored 175* vs Zimbabwe in 1983.
Ben Stokes was player of the match in the 2019 final.
